FAQ

+1. What is a Bug Bounty?
    Answer :

    A bug bounty is a reward offered by organizations to individuals who identify and report security vulnerabilities in their software or systems.

+How do I start as a Bug Bounty Hunter?
    Answer :

    Begin by signing up on a bug bounty platform, choosing programs that match your skills, and submitting detailed reports of any vulnerabilities you find.

+Are Bug Bounty Programs Safe?
    Answer :

    Yes, reputable bug bounty platforms ensure a secure and legal framework for researchers to report vulnerabilities responsibly.

+How Much Can I Earn from Bug Bounties?
    Answer :

    Earnings can vary widely based on the severity of the vulnerabilities discovered and the organization’s budget for bug bounties. Some researchers earn thousands of dollars per report.

+ Which Platform is Best for Beginners?
    Answer :

    Platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd are great for beginners due to their extensive resources and supportive communities.
