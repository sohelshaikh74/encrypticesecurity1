Mistake 3: Ignoring Chain of Custody

One of the biggest mistakes in network forensic investigation is to overlook the chain of custody. Evidence must be transferred in a documented and uninterrupted manner according to the chain of custody; if this is not maintained, doubts regarding the validity and dependability of the evidence may arise. Forensic analysis in cyber security and the evidence's admissibility in court depend on accurate recording of who handled the evidence and when.
