CEH v12 vs. Other Certifications

Comparison with CISSP:

While both CEH v12 and CISSP are prestigious certifications, CEH v12 focuses more on practical ethical hacking skills, whereas CISSP covers a broader range of cybersecurity management and policy topics. For hands-on penetration testing and hacking expertise, CEH v12 is the preferred choice.

Comparison with OSCP:

The CEH v12 certification provides a comprehensive overview of ethical hacking principles, ideal for those starting in the field. In contrast, OSCP is highly specialized and focuses on advanced penetration testing techniques. CEH v12 serves as a solid foundation before pursuing more advanced certifications like OSCP.
